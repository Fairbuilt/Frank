Client response
The Riverside Community Center will serve as a multi-generational hub for the East Riverside neighborhood. The facility should accommodate a range of activities including fitness classes, youth programs, senior services, and community events. Key goals include ADA accessibility throughout, natural light in major activity spaces, and a design that reflects the neighborhood's diverse cultural heritage. The existing site is approximately 1.8 acres and sits adjacent to Riverside Park. We would like to retain the existing mature oak trees on the north boundary if possible.
